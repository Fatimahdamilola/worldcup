Overview and their players

The portugal national football team has represented Portugal in international men's competition since 1921. The national team is controlled by the Portugese Football Federation, the governing body for football in Portugal. Portugal's home matches are played at various stadiums throughout Portugal, and its primary training ground and technical headquarters, Cidade do Futeboi, is located in Oeiras.
